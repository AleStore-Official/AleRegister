2.1 Data provided directly by the user

2.2 Data collected automatically

  • IP address

2.3 Data collected through third‑party services

Your data is processed for the following purposes:

  • Compliance with legal obligations

4. Legal Basis for Processing

  • User consent (e.g., registration, Google login)

We adopt appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or misuse. Authentication and database services are provided by Supabase, which implements industry‑standard security practices.

Supabase manages authentication, user accounts, and database storage. User passwords are encrypted and never accessible to AleStore.

Users may choose to log in using their Google account. Google provides only the information required for authentication (such as email and, when available, name).

reCAPTCHA protects the Service from automated abuse. Its use is subject to Google’s Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

6.4 Google Analytics

Analytics may be used to collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the Service. No personally identifiable information is tracked.
